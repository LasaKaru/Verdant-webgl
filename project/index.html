How To Play

W A S D Move  ·  MOUSE Aim (cursor = sights)
L-CLICK Fire (auto-locks to enemy)  ·  R-CLICK Aim  ·  R Reload
SHIFT Sprint  ·  SPACE Jump  ·  C Crouch  ·  G Grenade
F Enter / Exit Vehicle  ·  L Toggle Mouselook  ·  1-4 Weapon
E Pick Up  ·  TAB Inventory  ·  ESC Pause
M World Map  ·  B Black Market  ·  V Garage  ·  K Contracts

Aim with the mouse (cursor = your sights); push the cursor to a screen edge to turn. Grab cash drops, take contracts for bonus pay, and visit the 🅖 garage pad for a ride. Explore the valley & city, loot crates for ammo & medkits, and survive escalating waves of hostiles — with a boss every 5th wave.

Drive cars, hold the resupply pad to restock, and build kill streaks for a score multiplier. Harm civilians and your wanted level rises — police will hunt you until the heat cools off. Toggle weather (Clear/Rain/Auto) in Settings.
